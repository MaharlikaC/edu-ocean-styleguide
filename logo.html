Logo & Usage

Edu-Ocean Logo

Edu-Oceanâ€™s logo is of three fish swimming in a triangular formation, reminisant of the universal recycling symbol. The wake of the fish can be seen as either ocean water or branches of coral, forming a circle and further unifying the logo. The three fish represent the cornerstones of Edu-Ocean: Education, Preservation and The Ocean itself.

Correct Usage

The Edu-Ocean logo is not to be altered in any way from what is specified here.

  • The logo can have a black fill. Use on lighter coloured backgrounds.

  • The logo can also have white fill. Use against medium-dark coloured backgrounds

  • If using colour then the fish must be #0378A6(blue) and the waves can be #04BFAD(caribbean green).
    Use against lighter coloured backgrounds.

Incorrect Usage

  • The Edu-ocean logo should not be stretched width-wise, stretched length-wise, skewed, or rotated
