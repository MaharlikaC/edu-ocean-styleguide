Logo & Usage

Edu-Ocean Logo

Edu-Oceanâ€™s logo is of three fish swimming in a triangular formation, reminisant of the universal recycling symbol. The wake of the fish can be seen as either ocean water or branches of coral, forming a circle and further unifying the logo. The three fish represent the cornerstones of Edu-Ocean: Education, Preservation and The Ocean itself.

Correct Usage

The Edu-Ocean logo is not to be altered in any way from what is specified here.

The logo can have a black fill. Use on lighter coloured backgrounds. Each fish must be at a 45 degree angle.

If using colour then the fish must be #0378A6(blue) and the waves can be #04BFAD(caribbean green), use against lighter coloured backgrounds.

The logo can also be white for use against medium to dark coloured backgrounds





Incorrect Usage


The edu-ocean logo should not be stretched width wise, height wise, skewed, or rotated
