Typography



Open Sans

Use for Headings | 64 px | Bold

Open sans is a sans serif font used for the app's larger headings and titles. It is a highly legible due to the spacing of its characters. A connection can be drawn between the spacing of the characters to the vastness of the ocean.




Lato

Use for Sub Headings | 36 px | Normal

Lato is a sans serif font used in the app's various sub headings. This typeface is also high in legibility, which makes it a good pair with the Roboto typeface. Lato may not be familiar in name, but the cleanliness of this typeface demonstrates Edu-Ocean's goal for a cleaner ocean.



Roboto

Use for Regular/Paragraph Text | 24 px | Normal

Roboto is a sans serif font that has the highest legibility of the three chosen typefaces. The clarity that this typeface provides for userd is the same claritywe hope to see in our Oceans one day.
